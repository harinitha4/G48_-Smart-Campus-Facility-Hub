Late Penalty Rules

Penalties are calculated automatically based on your booking cancel time and check-in punctuality.

1) Late Booking Cancellation

Cancellation timing Penalty
Cancel within 2 hours of the booking start +5 points
Cancel after the booking start (or too late to matter) +10 points
Cancel 2 hours or more before start +0 points

2) Leaving the Booking Place Late

Exit timing Penalty
Leave 1–15 minutes late +3 points
Leave 16–30 minutes late +6 points
Leave more than 30 minutes late +10 points
Leave on time +0 points

Automatic System Behavior

When you cancel a booking, the system checks how close the cancellation is to the scheduled start time and adds the corresponding penalty points. When a booking ends, any recorded “actual exit” time can be evaluated the same way to apply leaving-late penalties.

Note: This page only shows the rules. Penalties are applied automatically during booking cancel/leave operations.
