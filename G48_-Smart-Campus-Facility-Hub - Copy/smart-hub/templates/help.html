How to Book a Facility

  • 1 Go to the Dashboard and browse available facilities.
  • 2 Click Book Now on the facility you want.
  • 3 Select your preferred date and time slot.
  • 4 Submit the booking and wait for admin approval.
  • 5 You'll receive a reminder notification 15 minutes before your booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?
Go to My Bookings and click the Cancel button next to the booking you want to remove. Cancellations can only be made before the booking starts.
Why is my booking status still "Pending"?
Bookings require admin approval. Please allow some time for the admin to review and approve your request. You can check the status in My Bookings.
When will I get a reminder notification?
Reminders are sent automatically 15 minutes before your booking start time. Check the Notifications page for all your alerts.
Can I edit my profile information?
Yes. Go to My Profile to update your display name, email, and other details.
What does "Limited" status mean?
"Limited" means the facility is nearly at full capacity. You may still be able to book, but slots are running out — book early!
